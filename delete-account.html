Delete Your NewsFlash Account

NewsFlash users can delete their account in the app from Profile > Settings > Delete account.

If you cannot access the app, email SUPPORT_EMAIL with the email address used for your NewsFlash account and request account deletion.

Deleting your account removes your profile, preferences, bookmarks, reading activity, notification settings, device tokens, and feedback linked to your account.

Some server logs or records required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance may be retained for a limited period.
