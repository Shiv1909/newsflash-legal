Privacy Policy

App: NewsFlash  |  Owner: Shivansh Mital  |  Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how NewsFlash ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using NewsFlash, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Usage data: Articles you open, like, or skip within the app. This helps us personalise your news feed.
  • Preferences: News categories and topics you select as interests.
  • Bookmarks: Articles you save for later reading.
  • Device token: A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send you push notifications. This is tied to your device, not your identity.
  • Feedback: Any message you voluntarily submit through the in-app feedback form.

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera data, or any sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the news experience (ranking articles based on your interests).
  • To sync your bookmarks and preferences across sessions.
  • To send you optional push notifications about top news stories (only if you enable them).
  • To respond to feedback you submit.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the app.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

NewsFlash uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase – Database and authentication. Your account data and app activity is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase – Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging). Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – Optional authentication method. If you use Google Sign-In, Google shares your name and email address with us. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/HTTPS). We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data using the "Delete Account" option in Settings.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time in Settings or your device's notification settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at shivanshmitalwo@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

NewsFlash is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

8. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.
